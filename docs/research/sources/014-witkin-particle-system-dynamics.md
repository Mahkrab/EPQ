# S014 — Witkin (1997)

## Full reference:

Witkin, A. (1997) *An Introduction to Physically Based Modeling: Particle System Dynamics*. Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University.

#### https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~baraff/pbm/particles.pdf

## Relevance:

Provides a clear foundation for representing particles as mass, position, velocity and force state, and for treating a particle system as a high-dimensional ODE suitable for numerical integration. This supports Maelstrom’s particle-simulation design and the report explanation of force accumulation, timestepping and interactive physically based modelling.

## Reliability:

Official Carnegie Mellon University course material by Andrew Witkin, a major researcher in physically based computer graphics. The notes are hosted from the CMU physically based modelling course page and form part of the Witkin, Baraff and Kass course-note sequence.
