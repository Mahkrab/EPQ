# S002 — Stam (1999)

## Full reference:

Stam, J. (1999) ‘Stable Fluids’, *Proceedings of the 26th Annual Conference on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques (SIGGRAPH ’99)*, pp. 121–128.

#### https://doi.org/10.1145/311535.311548

## Relevance:

Provides a contrasting Eulerian, grid-based method, and explains the relationship between numerical stability, timestep choice and interactive performance.

## Reliability:

Seminal SIGGRAPH paper, published through ACM. It is a primary research source and widely cited in graphics fluid simulation.
