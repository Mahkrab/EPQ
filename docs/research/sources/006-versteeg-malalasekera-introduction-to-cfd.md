# S006 — Versteeg and Malalasekera (2007)

## Full reference:

Versteeg, H.K. and Malalasekera, W. (2007) *An Introduction to Computational Fluid Dynamics: The Finite Volume Method*. 2nd edn. Harlow: Pearson Education.

#### https://books.google.com/books?id=RvBZ-UMpGzIC

## Relevance:

Explains continuum governing equations and finite-volume concepts, giving context for how engineering CFD differs from Maelstrom’s particle-focused simulation.

## Reliability:

Established CFD textbook, second edition, published by Pearson.
