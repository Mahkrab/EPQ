## Relevance:

Explains TCP ports, port numbers and firewalls, including the standard association between SSH and port 22. It supports the README’s port-forwarding and firewall terminology.

## Reliability:

Cloudflare is an established Internet infrastructure provider. The page gives a technically consistent secondary explanation and identifies IANA as the authority maintaining port assignments.

## Reading notes

- Ports identify services within a networked operating system.
- TCP and UDP carry port numbers at the transport layer.
- Port 22 is conventionally assigned to SSH.
